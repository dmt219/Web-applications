Sherlock Holmes

Bio

Early Life

I was born in 1854. My grandmother was sister to the French artist Vernet.

I had a brother named Mycroft, seven years older, who later was known as an established government official, and who played a large role in my story.

Life at Lehigh

Lehigh was where I first developed my method of deduction as an undergraduate. One of my fellow university students asked me to take the earliest case of my life

I spent six years after Lehigh as a consultant before meeting Dr. John H. Watson

Life with Watson

I and Watson stayed in an apartment at 221B Baker Street, London. I worked as a detective for twenty-three years, with Watson assisting me for seventeen.

I developed a strong sense of friendship with Dr. Watson
